Case Study

Keyword Research — Local Service Content

ClientLocal Service Brand (USA)
IndustryHome Services
Duration2 Months
ServiceKeyword Research

🔴 Problem

  • Blog had 50+ random articles with no structure
  • No keyword mapping — content didn't align with user intent
  • Low traffic (500 visitors/month)
  • No clear content strategy or calendar

🟡 Process

  • Conducted keyword research using Google Keyword Planner and Ubersuggest
  • Grouped 212 keywords by search intent (informational, commercial, transactional)
  • Mapped keywords to existing content and identified gaps
  • Created a 6-month content calendar with 30 blog topics
  • Prioritized high-volume, low-competition keywords

🟢 Outcome

212Keywords mapped
+147%Leads from organic
+200%Blog traffic

Traffic increased from 500 to 1500 visitors/month. Leads from organic search more than doubled.

📘 Learning

  • Intent mapping is critical — not all keywords are equal
  • Content gaps = opportunity gaps
  • A structured content calendar prevents random publishing
  • Low-competition keywords are gold for local businesses
