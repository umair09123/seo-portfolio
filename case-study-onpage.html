Case Study

On-Page SEO — E-commerce Product Pages

ClientE-commerce Store (USA)
IndustryRetail
Duration3 Months
ServiceOn-Page SEO

🔴 Problem

The client had 120+ product pages with thin content, duplicate meta descriptions, and keyword cannibalization. Many pages were ranking on page 3-4 with low CTR.

  • Thin content (less than 200 words per page)
  • Duplicate meta titles and descriptions across 40+ pages
  • Keyword cannibalization — multiple pages targeting same keyword
  • No internal linking strategy between related products

🟡 Process

I followed a structured on-page SEO audit and optimization process:

  • Audited all 120 product pages using Screaming Frog
  • Created unique meta titles and descriptions for each page
  • Expanded content to 300-500 words with relevant keywords
  • Fixed internal linking — added related product links
  • Implemented schema markup for products
  • Optimized image alt texts and file names

🟢 Outcome

+164%Organic traffic
+38Page 1 keywords
+45%CTR improvement

The client saw significant improvement within 3 months. Product pages moved from page 3-4 to page 1 for 38 keywords.

📘 Learning

  • Content depth matters — 300+ words per product page improved rankings
  • Unique meta titles and descriptions significantly boost CTR
  • Internal linking distributes link equity and improves crawlability
  • Schema markup helps Google understand product data better
