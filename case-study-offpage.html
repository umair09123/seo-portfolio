Case Study

Off-Page SEO — SaaS Brand Authority

ClientSaaS Brand (UK)
IndustrySoftware
Duration4 Months
ServiceOff-Page SEO

🔴 Problem

The client had a manual link penalty from Google due to low-quality backlinks. Domain rating dropped from 35 to 15. Organic traffic declined by 60%.

  • Manual link penalty from Google
  • 200+ toxic backlinks from spam sites
  • DR dropped from 35 to 15
  • 60% traffic loss in 2 months

🟡 Process

  • Conducted full backlink audit using Ahrefs
  • Created disavow file for 200+ toxic domains
  • Submitted reconsideration request to Google
  • Built 50+ quality backlinks via guest posts
  • Digital PR campaign targeting industry blogs

🟢 Outcome

+29Domain Rating
+92Referring domains
+50%Traffic recovered

Penalty removed within 4 weeks. DR recovered to 44. Traffic back to pre-penalty levels.

📘 Learning

  • Regular backlink audits prevent penalties
  • Quality over quantity — 10 good backlinks > 100 spam
  • Digital PR builds brand authority naturally
  • Disavow tool works if used correctly
